Hummus Toast

Description

Hummus on Toast is an extremely quick and easy meal. It can be enjoyed at home or on the go.

Ingredients

  • Store-bought hummus
  • Any bread

Steps

  1. Toast up your bread: Either in the toaster or toaster oven, if you’re making a small batch. Or if you happen to be making a large batch of hummus toast for a party, you can arrange slices of bread in a single layer on a baking sheet and toast them up in the oven.
  2. Spread on the hummus: Next, spread on your desired amount of hummus.
  3. Sprinkle on the toppings: Whatever you may be craving, then…
  4. Enjoy! Hummus toast is definitely best enjoyed right away. So dig in!
